About Myself

I am currently a fourth-year doctoral student at the Computational Robotics Lab(CRL) at ETH Zürich, advised by Prof. Stelian Coros and Prof. Bernhard Thomaszewski. I obtained my master's degree in Computer Graphics (CGGT) from the University of Pennsylvania with a thesis titled hybrid Lagrangian-Eulerian topology optimization under the supervision of Prof. Chenfanfu Jiang. My interests lie in physics-based simulation, PDE constrained optimization, computational design, and machine learning. CV
